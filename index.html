Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How long does a repair take?
Most common repairs — iPhone and Samsung screens or batteries — are done the same day, usually within about two hours. We'll give you an exact turnaround when you book.
Are you really cheaper than Mobile Klinik?
Yes. On comparable repairs we show both prices side by side, and we're consistently cheaper — up to $115 less on popular iPhone screen replacements. No mall-rent overhead means a lower price for you.
What's covered by the warranty?
Every repair includes a 90-day warranty on parts and labour. If something related to the repair fails in that window, we make it right.
Which devices do you repair?
iPhone, Samsung Galaxy phones and tablets, Google Pixel, and iPad — screens, batteries, back glass and charge ports. Search your exact model above for live pricing.
How do I book?
Find your device, open it, and tap Book on WhatsApp. Your message is pre-filled with the device and repair so we can confirm in seconds.
Will my data be safe?
Screen, battery, back glass and charge-port repairs don't require wiping your device. We recommend a backup before any repair as good practice, and we never access personal data.
