01 / Positioning

The honest, fast repair brand for Guelph

Nuera Tech fixes iPhone, Samsung, Pixel & iPad at transparent prices that beat other shops — same-day, 90-day warranty, booked over WhatsApp. The identity should feel like a well-funded consumer startup: confident, premium and approachable.

Audience

Who it's for

University of Guelph students, busy professionals and local residents who need their device back today — without the mall-chain runaround or surprise pricing.

Brand feel

How it should feel

Premium Trustworthy Fast Honest Local

12 / Direction

Where the mark goes next

The badge today is a clean geometric "N". The proposed direction — drawn from concepts the team liked — keeps the gradient DNA but makes the mark feel alive: a single continuous neon-tube stroke that doubles as a heartbeat / pulse line — "we bring your device back to life." A concept exploration, not final art.

Current

Geometric "N"

The mark shipping today — a confident, angular monogram in the purple→cyan gradient on an ink tile.

Proposed · concept

Pulse "N"

One unbroken neon stroke that reads as both the "N" and a heartbeat — warmer and more energetic, still unmistakably Nuera.

image-prompt · nuera-tech.txt 
Create a single premium editorial brand guideline and concept board for a real consumer tech-repair brand named Nuera Tech and its sub-brand family NueraCare, NueraExpress and NueraTrade.

Format: 16:9 landscape brand presentation board.

Brand feel: premium, trustworthy, modern, fast, approachable — a well-funded consumer startup. Dark, neon-on-ink aesthetic; vibrant but not gaming.

Audience: University of Guelph students, busy professionals and local Guelph residents who need fast, honest, affordable phone & tablet repair.

Visual identity: refined dark palette of ink black #07070c, brand purple #8b5cf6, brand cyan #22d3ee, action green #34d399, near-white #f4f6ff text and an amber #fbbf24 accent. Signature purple→cyan gradient. Include labeled colour swatches; keep all text minimal, clean and readable.

Design system to show:
- Main logo for Nuera Tech (gradient "N" mark + wordmark, "Tech" in cyan)
- Secondary lockups for NueraCare (green), NueraExpress (purple), NueraTrade (amber)
- Typography pairing: Inter (variable) for both the logo and supporting text
- Colour palette
- Simple line icons + playful doodles inspired by phone repair (screen, battery, charge port, spark, shield)
- Subtle aurora + film-grain patterns
- Product mockups: a repaired phone showing the app, a protective case, a NueraCare membership card, a NueraExpress same-day fast-lane ticket
- Packaging mockup: a branded repair-return box / device pouch
- Storefront signage: a glowing "Nuera Tech — Guelph" exterior sign + a window decal
- Interior / lifestyle mood: a clean, warm repair bench under soft neon glow
- Product photography-style hero: a device catching purple→cyan rim light on ink

Layout: clean modular sections, generous whitespace, rounded cards, premium editorial styling, consistent hierarchy, polished grid — not a messy collage.

Text to include:
Nuera Tech
NueraCare · NueraExpress · NueraTrade
Phone repair that's always cheaper.
Speed, trust & honest pricing.

Avoid: clutter, tiny unreadable text, misspelled brand names, too many random words, cheap Canva styling, overly cartoonish design, generic visuals, messy mockups, distorted packaging, or extra fake logos.
